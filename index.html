About CapeCredit

Transforming risk into opportunity for Cape Town's informal economy

The Problem

Traditional banks serve only 7% of informal businesses, with 76% rejected due to insufficient collateral. This leads to 70-80% business failure rates within the first 3 years.

Our Solution

CapeCredit provides a mobile-first platform with offline capability, alternative credit scoring that reduces default risk by 37-42%, and a complete pathway from informal to formal business status.

The Impact

Our approach creates 6,700-9,800 jobs (2.4x more than traditional approaches), enables R250M+ in new capital, and delivers 47% higher growth rates for participating businesses.
